Steps to Make Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Add tea leaves or a tea bag to a cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water into the cup.
  4. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add sugar, milk, or lemon as desired.
  6. Stir well and enjoy your tea!
